Inforrmation Technology Faculty

Prospective students from Jordan and abroad are invited to consider studying here. Faculty of Information Technology provides an attractive opportunity for students where they can find dedicated and competent faculty and staff, reasonable tuition fees, a vibrant academic atmosphere, and one of the most beautiful university campuses in Jordan.


Faculty of Information Technology covers a vast spectrum of topics which can be distinguished by different areas of applications. Our curriculum provides a common core of Computer Science \Computer Science, Computer Science\Computer Network, Computer Science\Multimedia Systems, Software Engineering, Computer Information Systems, and Cyber Security. Moreover, we are proud to be among the first universities in Jordan that offered the master program in software engineering.


Our mission is to prepare students for positions of leadership in industry, government, and academia by empowering them with computer scientist knowledge with skills and experience in the following areas: Computer Application, Computer Networking and Security, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Graphics, User Interfaces, Computer Simulation, Software Engineering, and Computer Information Systems.


You are invited to browse through our website to be familiar with our programs and people. If you have any inquiries, please do not hesitate to contact us.
